This tool will import the Cold Blood adventure content into your world. If the content already exists, it will be updated.

Content to Import:

  • Scenes: Battlemap (with Lighting, Walls, Regions)
  • Actors: Cryovain, Grimjaw, Water Weird, Mira, Aldric
  • Journals: Full Adventure Guide
  • Items: Quest Rewards & MacGuffins

Note: This may overwrite changes you've made to these specific documents if they share the same ID.
